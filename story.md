# Shadow Runner — Story

## Prologue: The Vanishing Light

Long ago, the kingdom of **Lumeria** was protected by an ancient magical force known as the **Three Lumens** — crystals that balanced light, shadow, and life.

One night, the Lumens vanished.

Without them, darkness slowly began spreading through the world. Strange creatures appeared, villages became abandoned, and an unknown force started controlling the shadows themselves.

You play as **Aren**, a quiet knight trained in the ancient art of **Shadow Running** — the ability to move unseen through darkness.

Your mission begins when an old villager named **Elder Bran** reveals the truth:

> _“Someone has awakened the Shadow Engine… and it feeds on fear.”_

To stop it, you must locate the missing Lumens.

But the deeper you go, the more the shadows start **watching you back.**

---

# Level 1 — The Forgotten Village (Bluish-Green Map)

### Setting

A moss-covered abandoned settlement at dusk. Blue-green tones give it a mysterious twilight feel.

Old houses crumble, lanterns flicker, and slimes roam the pathways.

### Story Purpose

Tutorial level + story introduction.

### Key NPC

**Elder Bran**

AI dialogue example:

- If player approaches slowly:
  _“Quiet steps… good. The slimes sense vibrations.”_

- If player keeps dying:
  _“Shadow Runner… patience wins battles that swords cannot.”_

### Gameplay Story

Elder Bran explains:

- The **First Lumen** was hidden beneath the village.
- Creatures appeared after a **portal opened in the forest**.

You must sneak past patrol slimes and activate an ancient portal.

### Ending Event

You reach the **first glowing portal**.

Elder Bran says:

> “The desert ruins hold the next truth… but beware, traveler.
> The shadows there… whisper.”

Portal activates → Level transition.

---

# Level 2 — The Sunken Desert Ruins (Yellow Map)

### Setting

A desert where ancient structures are buried under sand.

Color palette: yellow, amber, dusty ruins.

Wind blows constantly and half-broken temples appear.

### Story Purpose

Reveal the **true antagonist**.

### New NPC Type

**Echo Spirits** — AI NPC ghosts trapped in ruins.

They respond dynamically:

If player asks questions:

> “We guarded the Lumen once… until the **Architect** came.”

If player runs carelessly:

> “You move loudly… the guardians will hear.”

### Gameplay Story

You discover ancient writings explaining:

- A scientist-mage called **The Architect** attempted to **control shadows using machines**.
- His experiment created the **Shadow Engine**.

But the engine corrupted him.

He now controls monsters through the shadows.

### Level Boss Moment (Stealth Escape)

A massive **purple slime guardian** patrols the ruins.

Instead of fighting, the player must:

- hide
- move between pillars
- trigger mechanisms

### Ending Discovery

You find the **Second Lumen fragment**.

When touched, the fragment whispers:

> “The final light waits in the **Crimson Bloom**.”

Portal opens to next level.

---

# Level 3 — The Crimson Bloom (Pink Map)

### Setting

A strange glowing forest where pink crystals grow from trees.

It looks beautiful but unnatural.

Shadows move **independently of objects**.

### Story Purpose

Reveal that the shadows themselves are now alive.

### New Enemy Behavior

Enemies **react dynamically** using AI:

Examples:

If player hides often:

> “I know you are there, little knight…”

If player moves fast:

> “RUN… run faster… it makes the hunt exciting.”

This makes the world feel **alive and threatening**.

### NPC

A corrupted AI NPC called **The Watcher**.

It was once a guardian spirit but now speaks cryptically.

Example dialogue:

> “The Architect tried to control shadows…
> but shadows prefer freedom.”

### Gameplay

Player navigates through:

- moving shadow walls
- hidden paths
- enemy patrols that adapt behavior

### Final Discovery

You reach the **core of the Shadow Engine**.

There you finally meet **The Architect**.

But he is no longer human.

He has become a **living shadow entity**.

He says:

> “Heroes swing swords.
> But shadows… only survive.”

The player cannot defeat him yet.

Instead, you escape after stealing the **Third Lumen**.

# Future Levels (Expansion Potential)

## Level 4 — The Shadow City

Enemies communicate with each other.

NPCs might lie to the player.

AI becomes more psychological.

---

## Level 5 — The Engine Core

Player infiltrates the heart of the Shadow Engine.

Stealth becomes extremely difficult.

Enemies predict movement.

---

## Final Level — Eclipse

All Lumens restored.

You confront **The Architect** inside a collapsing shadow dimension.

Two endings possible:

### Ending 1 — Restore Light

Destroy the Shadow Engine.

Shadows return to normal.

Peace returns.

---

### Ending 2 — Become the Shadow Runner

You realize shadows cannot be destroyed.

You take control of the engine.

You become the **new guardian of darkness**.
